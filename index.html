How to edit

  • Switch vans with the dropdown — dimensions and the wheel-arch band update to that model's real interior specs.
  • Click and drag any block by its body to move it.
  • Drag the small dark square in a block's corner to resize it.
  • Live size in cm shows below each label while you resize.
  • The tan shaded band is the wheel-arch intrusion zone — cabinets crossing it need to be shallower there.
  • Cab and rear-door blocks are fixed in place (front/back walls).
  • "Reset layout" restores the suggested arrangement for the current van.
